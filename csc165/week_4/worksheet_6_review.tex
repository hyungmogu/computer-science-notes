    \bigskip

    \bigskip

    \textbf{Correct Solution:}

    $IsGCD(x,y,d):\exists x,y,d \in \mathbb{Z}, (x = 0 \land y = 0 \Rightarrow d = 0) \land (x \neq 0 \lor y \neq 0 \Rightarrow
